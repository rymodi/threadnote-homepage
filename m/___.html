You can create a bookmark that will send the address of the current website to Threadnote.

Do do so, take the following steps:

  1. Bookmark this page: In Safari on your iOS device, tap the button, tap Add Bookmark, and tap Save.
  2. Edit the bookmark: Tap the icon, tap Edit, and select your bookmark. In the second field, delete everything and replace with the following:
javascript:window.location='threadnote://new?text='+escape(window.location)

You can now send the address of the website you are viewing to Threadnote by selecting this bookmark from the bookmarks menu.
